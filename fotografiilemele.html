HOTEL NIXE -CALA MAJOR

CALA MAJOR HOTEL NIXE (.... back ....to 1872,.... when Archduke Louis Salvador of Austria (the second cousin with Emperor Franz Joseph) arrived in Mallorca aboard the Nixe, and was fascinated by landscapes, nature and the people from the Balearic Islands. ....aristocrat, writer, painter and illustrator.....Nixe , his ship was anchored for many years in the bay of Cala Major,.....and the hotel takes its name. ....The legendary figure of the Nixe is the mermaid found on the ship, whose job was to ensure good luck on voyages.....today we can say that , Nixe continues at full sail on the turquoise sea of Cala Major..
